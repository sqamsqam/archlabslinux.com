ArchLabs Linux is an Arch-based distro, influenced and inspired by the look and feel of BunsenLabs with the intermediate to advanced user in mind.

With the rolling release cycle of Arch Linux, new packages will be available immediately, making upgrades a thing of the past.

The ArchLabs ISO does not include a live environment. You are straight into the console. The installer is launched from there.
